## Bugs Fixed

1. **Sulfate double-counting**: Both SU bin 001 and bin 002 read the same `SO4` mass field from the GEOS-IT inst file but applied different optics radii, counting sulfate mass twice. Fixed by using a single radius bin (radius[0]), matching GEOS-IT.

2. **OCPHILIC wrong radius bin**: OCPHILIC used `idx_size=0` (radius[0]), which has no RH dependence (bext constant at all humidities). The correct assignment is `idx_size=1` (radius[1]), which has hygroscopic growth (bext increases from 2672 at RH=0 to 7008 at RH=80% to 16044 at RH=95%).

3. **Dust optics version**: Updated from v14_2 to v15_3 per GMAO recommended versions.

## Residual Bias Sources

The remaining -2.6% total bias is attributable to:

- **Temporal sampling**: inst timestamps (T0000, T0300, ...) vs tavg windows (T0130, T0430, ...). Contributes ~0 to the daily mean bias (cancels over the day) but adds ~0.01 per-pixel noise.
- **Regridding**: subsampling from 576x361 to 288x180 smooths sharp AOD gradients. Contributes < 0.0001 to the global mean.
- **Computation path**: our species-by-species optics + RH interpolation vs GEOS-IT's internal AOD calculation. This is the dominant residual.
